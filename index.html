About Me

I hold a Ph.D. from XLRI Jamshedpur, specializing in Entrepreneurship and Innovation. With 17 years of corporate experience, I have held senior strategic roles at organizations such as Wipro, Accenture, Cognizant, and Mahindra.

My research focuses on entrepreneurial resourcefulness, emphasizing Neurodiversity, Dynamic Capabilities, Frugal Innovation, and Family Business Dynamics. It explores how businesses creatively leverage limited resources to drive growth, embodying the spirit of 'jugaad' in resource-constrained environments.

I am an advocate for continuous learning and innovation, fostering a collaborative and mentoring-oriented approach in both academia and professional spaces. I hold a B.Tech from Utkal University and an MBA from Xavier Institute of Management, Bhubaneswar (XIMB).
