About Me

Swapnil Sahoo holds a Ph.D. from XLRI Jamshedpur, specializing in Entrepreneurship and Innovation. With 17 years of extensive corporate experience, he has held senior strategic roles at leading organizations such as Wipro, Accenture, Cognizant, Mahindra, and most recently, as Global Partnership Head at 42Gears Mobility Pvt Ltd. In these roles, he excelled in building relationships with C-suite executives and decision-makers across various industries in Europe, the Middle East, and Africa region.

My research investigates entrepreneurial resourcefulness, exploring how organizations innovate and thrive within constraints—a concept central to modern strategic thinking in both emerging and developed markets.
