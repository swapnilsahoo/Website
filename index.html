About Me

With a Ph.D. from XLRI Jamshedpur and 17 years of corporate experience at firms like Wipro and Accenture, my work exists at the intersection of academic theory and strategic practice.

My research investigates entrepreneurial resourcefulness, exploring how organizations innovate and thrive within constraints—a concept central to modern strategic thinking in both emerging and developed markets.
